Phase 1 Logo Concepts · Brick / Pixel-Block Lane

dbx.tools coral brick system

Four nerdy, digital logo directions for open-source-style Databricks developer tooling. Each concept pairs a crisp brick/pixel mark with a geometric sans wordmark and an extremely subtle mustache hidden in the mark.

Primary #F86C5A Hot Coral #FF7A5C Dark Coral #C94E44 Tint #FFF0EA

1 · Pixel d Stack

A lowercase d assembled from coral bricks, tuned for favicon clarity; the tiny negative-space double notch cut into the right-side bowl bricks is the mustache.

2 · Isometric Build Blocks

A stacked-cube mark with Databricks-adjacent geometry without copying it; the mustache is a quiet curved cut between the lower cubes.

3 · Block Wrench

A developer-tool glyph made from square bricks, with the wrench jaw abstracted into pixels; the mustache hides as paired notches in the jaw gap.

4 · Terminal Brick Cursor

A pixel chevron plus brick cursor for CLI/package energy; the mustache is the near-invisible two-dot cut inside the cursor brick.

All SVGs are vector-only with explicit viewBox values, coral variants for light and near-black surfaces, and pixel-aligned brick geometry for small-size rendering.
